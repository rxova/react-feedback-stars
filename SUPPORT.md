# Support

- **Bug?** Open a [bug report](https://github.com/jonakrusze/react-feedback-stars/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml).
- **Idea or usage question?** Start a
  [discussion](https://github.com/jonakrusze/react-feedback-stars/discussions).
- **Security issue?** Follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) — please do not open a public issue.

Before filing a bug, it helps enormously to include the `precision` / `rounding` combination and
the icon type you are using. Those two axes account for most reported behaviour differences.
